Organize Your PDF Pages

Merge, split, remove, reorder, rotate and resize pages. Everything runs in your browser — files never leave your device.

🔗

Merge PDF iUpload 2 or more PDFs and drag to reorder. Downloads as a single merged PDF.

Combine multiple PDFs into one file

→
✂️

Split PDF iEnter ranges like "1-3, 5, 7-9" to split into multiple PDFs. Each range becomes a separate file.

Split into separate files or page ranges

→
🗑️

Remove Pages iSee visual thumbnails of all pages. Click any page to mark it for deletion, then apply.

Click thumbnails to mark & delete pages

→
↕️

Reorder Pages iDrag page thumbnails to any position. The order you see is what gets saved in the PDF.

Drag thumbnails to rearrange order

→
🔄

Rotate Pages iSelect specific pages or all pages. Choose rotation: 90° right, 90° left, or 180°.

Rotate pages 90°, 180° or 270°

→
🔁

Reverse Pages iInstantly reverses the entire page order. The last page becomes the first, and so on.

Flip the entire page order instantly

→
📌

Extract Pages iSelect pages by clicking thumbnails or typing page numbers. Selected pages are saved as a new PDF.

Pull specific pages into a new PDF

→
📐

Resize Pages iChange all pages to A4, A3, Letter, Legal or enter custom dimensions in mm or points.

Change dimensions: A4 ↔ Letter ↔ custom
